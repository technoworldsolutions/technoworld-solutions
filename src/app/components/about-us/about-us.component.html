About TechnoWorld Solutions

Founded in 2019 and fully operational since February 4, 2020, TechnoWorld Solutions is a premier precision metal fabrication workshop based in Chalisgaon, Dist. Jalgaon (MH). We take pride in our mastery of laser cutting, fabrication, and custom metalwork, delivering impeccable quality with every intricate cut. Serving both residential and industrial clients, our dedication and care shine through in every project we undertake. We are committed to delivering excellence and innovation in our every project.
